    About US


    A group of young boys from Kashipur organized a franchise-based short-hand flash ball cricket tournament named the Kashipur Premier League in 2017. In Maiden's year, Crazy Boys, one of the successful franchises, beat BBSS in the final and lifted the first trophy. Because of some unavoidable circumstances, KPL was postponed in 2018 and 2019. In 2020, which was infamous because of COVID-19, a group of boys from Kashipur, in collaboration with Jonaki Sangha, took another initiative to revive the game , brand value and the name of the tournament, and they did it. In 2020 season 2, Rudrapur Sabuj Sangha, which was also a successful franchise, beat CB in the final and lifted the trophy. In 2021, in season 3, CB beats Rudrapur Sabuj Sangha in the final. Season 4, 2022, saw a new franchise and a new winner named ABBYS, who beat HDDF in a close encounter. After successfully completing four seasons, we are heading toward season 5, jointly organized by Jonaki Sangha and the KPL core team. Season 5 will be much bigger and happening; 10 teams are going to take part in this edition. This year, we have two new team which are United Ancholik Dol jointly owned by Dr.Sukanta Mondal and Er.Sajal Biswas ,Titan Super Kings jointly owned by Subhajit Mitra and Rahul Ghosh , and five old franchises BBSS (one of the oldest team since 2017) solely owned by Gautam Baidya , 11 Spartan (one of the oldest team since 2017) jointly owned by Subrata Biswas and Tanmay Biswas ,Long Live Mad Max (since 2020) jointly owned by Linkan Biswas and Dip Sarkar , ABBYS (since 2022) jointly owned by Samir Bala and Pabitra Howlader and Kashipur (since 2022) jointly owned by Ramani Baul and Raju Baul and the Owner of HDDF Tanup Mallick has tranferred his team To Kishore Mondal and Animesh Mandal for this year and they are coming up with a new name Vikings, same goes here for Rudrapur Sabuj Sangha where ownership has been transfered to Pankaj Bala and Prosanta Bala and they came up with a new name RSS and Rising Star has changed their name to Joi Pagaol where Roni Mallick and Tapas Roy has the joint ownership
